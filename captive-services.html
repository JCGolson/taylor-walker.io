Captive Insurance

Taylor-Walker Consulting, LLC has been providing actuarial services to captive insurance companies since 1993. Being one of the first actuarial firms to become involved with the captive industry, we have had the unique opportunity of helping in its growth. As such, we have learned what will make or break a newly formed company.

We have consulted on both sides of a captive company formation, performing feasibility studies for the prospective company, and also reviewing captive applications and performing financial examinations for state insurance departments. Years of being involved with this industry has given us experience with various types of formations and coverages.

We are approved actuaries in most major on-shore and many off-shore domiciles.
