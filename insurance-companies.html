Insurance Companies

Taylor-Walker Consulting, LLC consults with insurance companies, and self-funded entities. We conduct quarterly and year-end reserve analyses for all types of insurance companies and self-funded plans. We also prepare statutory and other required reserve opinions. Additionally, we conduct annual premium rating and experience studies, and recommend funding levels for self-insured groups, among our services.
