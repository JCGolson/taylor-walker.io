SCOTT S. GARDUNO

FSA, MAAA

Present Position

Managing Member and Senior Health and Life Consulting Actuary

Responsibilities

Administers, coordinates, and supervises Taylor-Walker’s services. Also provides health and life actuarial consulting services to regulatory and industry clients. Conducts risk-focused actuarial examinations; reconciles and analyzes claims and claims adjustment expense data; tests reserve factors; reviews and calculates actuarial assets, policy and claims reserves, and other actuarial liabilities; reviews cash flow and asset adequacy analyses; evaluates reinsurance contracts, coverages, and risk transfer; determines rate and surplus adequacy; analyzes rate filings; reviews captive and risk retention group applications and actuarial opinions; conducts actuarial analyses for companies in receivership; and prepares actuarial opinions, reports, and workpapers.

Has expertise in conducting reserving and rating analyses for health maintenance organizations, Blue Cross Blue Shield companies, multiple employer welfare arrangements, captive insurance companies and risk retention groups, and self-insured plans.

Also provides property and casualty actuarial consulting services.

Experience

Managing Member of Taylor-Walker Consulting, LLC since November 19, 2015. Began working for its predecessor company, Taylor-Walker & Associates, Inc., as an Actuarial Analyst in November 2006, progressing to Senior Health and Life Consulting Actuary.

Conducts reserve, experience accounting, and ratemaking analyses for insurance companies, captives, and self-insured groups, including the following:

  • BevCap Health Protected Cell, LLC
  • Card &Card Insurance Company
  • Community Hospital Alternative for Risk Transfer, A RRG
  • Laclede Insurance Risk Services, Inc.
  • Landcar Life Insurance Company
  • Salt Lake Community College
  • Total Dental Administrators Health Plan, Inc.
  • Total Dental Administrators of Utah, Inc.
  • Wyoming Association of Municipalities

Mr. Garduno is the Appointed Actuary for nine companies including BevCap Health Protected Cell, LLC; Laclede Insurance Risk Services, Inc.; Landcar Life Insurance Company; the Total Dental Administrators companies; and four other captive insurance companies.

Supervises and conducts numerous NAIC risk-focused actuarial examinations for state insurance regulators. A sampling of the companies Mr. Garduno has examined is listed below:

  • AF&L Insurance Company
  • American Life & Accident Insurance Company of Kentucky
  • Essence Healthcare, Inc.
  • Highmark Group of Companies
  • Montana Dental Association Group Benefits Trust, a MEWA

Mr. Garduno also conducts property and casualty feasibility, reserve, and funding studies, and prepared property and casualty actuarial reserve analyses for two companies in rehabilitation.

Previously, worked as an Actuarial Analyst for Milliman, Inc. for three years. Projected claims costs and estimated IBNR reserves for use in health insurance premium calculations, and performed actuarial valuations for pension plans.
